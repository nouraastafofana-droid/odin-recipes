Banana peanut Butter snack

Description

A very simple and satisfying snack that combines natural sweetness with healthy fats and protein. Perfect when you need something quick and comforting !

Ingredients

For this recipe, you'll need:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • A few nuts or seeds
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Peel the banana.
  2. Cut it into slices.
  3. Place the slices in a bowl or on a plate.
  4. Add peanut butter on top.
  5. Sprinkle nuts or seeds if desired.
  6. Add a little cinnamon for extra flavor.
  7. Enjoy your meal !
